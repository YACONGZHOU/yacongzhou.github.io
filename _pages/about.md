Hi there, welcome to my page! I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the Shenzhen Institutes of Advanced Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences. My research is in combinatorics.

I received my PhD in Computer Science from Royal Holloway, University of London, under the supervision of [Gregory Gutin](https://pure.royalholloway.ac.uk/en/persons/gregory-gutin/) and [Stefanie Gerke](https://pure.royalholloway.ac.uk/en/persons/stefanie-gerke/). Before that, I completed my master's degree at Shanghai University, supervised by [Liying Kang](https://en.math.shu.edu.cn/info/1017/1586.htm).
